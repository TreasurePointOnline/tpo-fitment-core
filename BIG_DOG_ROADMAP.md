# Big Dog Roadmap for Treasure Point Online (TPO)

This roadmap outlines the steps for developing the core content pages based on standard e-commerce best practices, aligning with the "Big Dog" strategy for a high-end car audio site.

## I. Homepage Content Development

The homepage is critical for first impressions, brand messaging, and guiding users. It needs a compelling hero section and SEO-rich content to rank well.

### A. Hero Section
1.  **Objective:** Immediately engage visitors and communicate value proposition.
2.  **Elements:**
    *   **High-Quality Visual:** A captivating image or short video showcasing premium car audio, installations, or lifestyle.
    *   **Headline:** Clear, concise, and benefit-driven (e.g., "Experience Unrivaled Car Audio," "Precision Sound for Your Ride").
    *   **Sub-headline/Description:** Elaborate on the unique selling proposition (e.g., "Curated Selection, Expert Installation, Unmatched Performance").
    *   **Call-to-Action (CTA):** Prominent button(s) leading to key sections (e.g., "Shop Amplifiers," "Explore Subwoofers," "Find Your Fitment").
    *   **Vehicle Fitment Search (tpo-fitment-core integration):** Integrate the `tpo-fitment-core` plugin's search widget prominently, allowing users to quickly find products compatible with their vehicle.

### B. Homepage Body Content (SEO & User Engagement)
1.  **Objective:** Provide valuable information for users, establish authority, and improve search engine rankings.
2.  **Sections:**
    *   **Featured Categories/Products:** Dynamically display top-selling products, new arrivals, or specific categories with high-quality images and short descriptions.
    *   **Brand Highlights:** Showcase premium brands carried by TPO, reinforcing the "high-end" positioning. Include brand logos and links to brand-specific pages.
    *   **Why Choose TPO?**
        *   **Expertise:** Highlight knowledge in car audio, installation, and tuning.
        *   **Quality Assurance:** Emphasize curated selection, genuine products, and warranty support.
        *   **Customer Service:** Detail support options, return policies, and satisfaction guarantee.
    *   **Customer Testimonials/Reviews:** Social proof to build trust and credibility.
    *   **Blog/Latest News Snippets:** Showcase recent articles, guides, or industry news to demonstrate thought leadership and encourage content consumption.
    *   **SEO Text Block:**
        *   A well-written, keyword-rich paragraph or two at the bottom of the page that describes TPO, its offerings, target audience, and key product categories.
        *   Avoid keyword stuffing; focus on natural language that provides value to both users and search engines.
        *   Include internal links to important category pages or guides.
    *   **Newsletter Signup:** Encourage email subscriptions for promotions and updates.

## II. Trust Pages Content Development

These pages are essential for building customer confidence, addressing common concerns, and complying with legal requirements.

### A. Privacy Policy
1.  **Objective:** Inform users about data collection, usage, storage, and protection practices.
2.  **Key Sections (based on GDPR, CCPA, etc.):**
    *   **Introduction:** Who TPO is and commitment to privacy.
    *   **Information We Collect:**
        *   Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Name, address, email, phone.
        *   Payment Information (how it's handled, e.g., "processed by secure third-party").
        *   Usage Data: IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, device info.
        *   Cookies & Tracking Technologies.
    *   **How We Use Your Information:** Order processing, customer support, personalization, marketing (with consent), improving services.
    *   **How We Share Your Information:** Third-party service providers (shipping, payment gateways), legal requirements.
    *   **Your Rights:** Access, correction, deletion, objection to processing (Data Subject Rights).
    *   **Data Security:** Measures taken to protect data.
    *   **Retention:** How long data is kept.
    *   **Children's Privacy:** Policy regarding users under a certain age.
    *   **Changes to This Policy:** How users will be notified.
    *   **Contact Information:** For privacy inquiries.

### B. Warranty Information
1.  **Objective:** Clearly outline the terms and conditions for product warranties, providing transparency and managing customer expectations.
2.  **Key Sections:**
    *   **Introduction:** TPO's commitment to quality and manufacturer warranties.
    *   **General Warranty Statement:** All products sold come with the manufacturer's warranty. TPO does not provide direct warranties unless explicitly stated for specific services or products.
    *   **Manufacturer Warranties:**
        *   Explanation that warranty terms vary by manufacturer.
        *   Encourage customers to check individual product pages or manufacturer websites for specific details.
        *   Provide links or instructions on how to find manufacturer warranty information.
    *   **Warranty Claim Process:**
        *   Step-by-step guide on how customers can initiate a warranty claim (e.g., contact TPO customer service, provide proof of purchase, details of defect).
        *   What information is required (order number, product serial, description of issue).
    *   **Exclusions:** What voids a warranty (e.g., improper installation, physical damage, unauthorized repairs, normal wear and tear).
    *   **Returns vs. Warranty:** Clarify the difference between TPO's return policy and manufacturer warranty claims.
    *   **Limitation of Liability:** Standard legal disclaimer.
    *   **Contact Information:** For warranty inquiries.
